
### Netflix Data Visualization Dashboard analysis
This Tableau dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of Netflix movies and TV shows, exploring trends in genres, ratings, release years, and content distribution across countries.
### Dashboard Features
1. Movies and TV Shows Distribution
Visualizes the percentage of Movies vs. TV Shows on Netflix.

2. Ratings Analysis
Displays the count of content for each rating (e.g., PG, TV-MA, R).

3. Top 10 Genres
Shows the most common genres across all titles.

4. Geographical Distribution
A world map displaying how Netflix content is spread across countries.

5. Yearly Trend
A timeline showing the growth of movies and TV shows added to Netflix from 2008 to 2020.

6. Interactive Filters
Filter by Type (Movie/TV Show) and Title to dynamically explore detailed info such as:
Ratings
Release Year
Genre
Duration
Date Added

## Project Insights – Netflix Data Analysis Dashboard
### 1. Content Type Distribution
Movies make up approximately 68.41% of all titles, while TV Shows account for 31.59%.This indicates that Netflix’s library is still largely dominated by movies, although TV Shows have grown in recent years.
 ### 2. Geographical Distribution
The United States has the highest number of titles on Netflix, followed by India, United Kingdom, and Canada.Netflix has expanded its content globally, with a strong representation in countries across Europe and Asia, showcasing its diverse international catalog.The dominance of the US suggests that most original productions and licensed content still originate there.
### 4. Genre Insights (Top 10 Genres)
The most popular genres on Netflix include.
International Movies
Dramas
Comedies
Documentaries
Action & Adventure
### 5. Ratings Distribution
The majority of content is rated TV-MA and TV-14, meaning much of Netflix’s content is targeted at mature and teen audiences.Family-friendly content (G, PG) forms a smaller portion of the catalog, showing Netflix’s focus on adult and young-adult demographics.
### 6. Duration and Content Length
Movies have a wide duration range, with many falling around 90–150 minutes.TV Shows vary in the number of seasons, but the most common are limited series (1–2 seasons).
### Overall Summary
Netflix’s library is expanding globally with strong regional representation.

Movies dominate the content base, but TV Shows are steadily increasing.

The service focuses on drama-driven, mature-rated content.

The 2015–2019 period marks Netflix’s golden growth era, with massive content addition across multiple genres.
